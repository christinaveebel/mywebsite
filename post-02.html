How to make sushi?

Step 1Prep the Rice & Nori. Lay out the nori on the bamboo mat (or whatever you're using). Step 2Spread the Rice on the Nori. Step 3Add Fillings to the Bottom Quarter of the Nori. Step 4Roll the Sushi Part Way. Step 5Roll the Sushi the Rest of the Way. Step 6Move to Cutting Board. Step 7Cut the Sushi. Step 8Serve.
